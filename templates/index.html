Why NETA
Built on Trust, Designed for India
🔐
Biometric Identity
Every voter is verified using their Aadhaar-linked fingerprint. No impersonation. No proxy voting. One person, one vote — guaranteed.
🗳️
Secret Ballot
Your vote is cryptographically separated from your identity. Even system administrators cannot link who you are to what you voted.
⛓️
Tamper-Proof Ledger
Every vote is stored with an integrity hash. Any modification is immediately detectable, ensuring the final count is mathematically verifiable.
🏠
Vote from Anywhere
Citizens in remote areas, the elderly, and differently-abled voters can participate from any registered device — eliminating booth travel barriers.
📋
Full Audit Trail
Every system action — login, verification, vote cast — is logged with timestamp and IP. Identity actions are auditable; vote choices never are.
⚡
Real-Time Results
Live tallying as polls close. No counting delays. Election Commission officials see accurate, certified results the moment voting ends.
The Process
Four Steps to Your Vote
01
Aadhaar Lookup
Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar. The system verifies it against the encrypted national voter roll within milliseconds.
02
Fingerprint Scan
Place your finger on the sensor. Your biometric template is matched — confirming you are physically present and authorised.
03
Select Candidate
View your constituency's ballot. Choose your candidate — or NOTA. Your selection is encrypted the moment you confirm.
04
Vote Recorded
Your encrypted vote is stored. A unique cryptographic receipt is issued. You cannot vote again — and no one can change your vote.

Security Architecture
Zero Trust.
Total Integrity.

NETA is designed on the principle that no single component — not the server, not the database, not even the administrator — should ever be trusted implicitly. Every action is verified, every record is cryptographically signed.

  • Identity and vote are stored in separate, isolated databases with no foreign key link
  • Each vote generates a unique SHA-256 hash — your verifiable proof of participation
  • Atomic database transactions prevent partial writes or race conditions
  • All biometric data is hashed and never stored in plain form
  • Session tokens are invalidated immediately after vote is cast
  • Full audit log captures every system event — without revealing vote choices
NETA
